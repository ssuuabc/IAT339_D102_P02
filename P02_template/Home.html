Why we matter

Extra Cushioning

Feature Latex foam at three critical places: insole at strike contact, under the ball of the foot and full length of the lining. Absorbs shock, supports body weight and distributes pressure.

Balanced Heel To Toe

Heel shapes are designed for stability while the inside heel cup provides a secure fit. Promotes a stable heel strike & toe-off which enables a natural gait.

Flexible Soles

Flexible materials used in the insole & outsole. Relieves pressure & allows for a more natural toe-off
